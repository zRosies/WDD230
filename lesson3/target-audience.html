Target Audience

Potential investors, entrepreneurs, and businesses in the tourism industry who are looking for opportunities to expand their operations or invest in new ventures. It may also include individuals and organizations interested in promoting economic development in the region, such as government agencies, non-profits, and community groups. Also, tourists and travelers who are interested in visiting Rio de Janeiro and the surrounding area may also be a part of the target audience, as they could be potential customers for the businesses that are being promoted through the website.

Persona 1

Name: Sarah

Occupation: Writer

Age: 30

Location: New York, USA

Background:

Sarah is a freelance writer who loves to travel and explore new destinations. She has visited several countries in Europe, Asia, and Africa, but has never been to South America. Sarah is planning a trip to Rio de Janeiro with her husband, and is looking for information about the city, its attractions, and the best places to stay. She is willing to find information about the most-seen attractions.

Goals

  • Find information about the must-see attractions in Rio de Janeiro.
  • Discover local restaurants and bars that offer authentic Brazillian cuisine.
  • Find accommodations at strategic city points that may offer easy access to the city's main attractions.

Concerns

  • Difficulty finding reliable information about the safety of the city.
  • Concerns about language barriers and communication difficulties.
  • Limited knowledge of the local culture and customs.
