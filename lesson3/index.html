Rio360 - Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

Our website plan is to increase investments in tourism in the marvelous city of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Rio de Janeiro is the most visited city in Brazil, being the tourist destination known for its beaches, and landmarks such as the ‘Christ the Redeemer’ and ‘Sugarloaf Mountain’, the state contains many beautiful cities such as Arraial do Cabo, Rio das Ostras, and wonderful places worth to visit which is its essential characteristics. Bringing investors to this field would make a huge difference in their business increasing their revenue and amplifying their investments by promoting overall economic development in a region where tourism is constantly on the rise, developing jobs opportunities in the tourism area.
